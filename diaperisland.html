Trapped on Diaper Island? Go Home.

Here's me singing to my son as I taught him to use the potty. The song morphed into a bedtime story and, with the help of his older brother, into an action adventure equipped with 3D models sculpted from his original drawings.

Like, what if the only diaper around was forever clean, where would you go? That's the question on Diaper Island.

The question now is, why give this kinda hand-drawn effort when this very website is vibe-coded? Who knows. Just believe in drawing. Anywho, we had fun, and we made something together.

We laughed about poop, and enshallah it'll help you potty train your child.

And if your kid already knows how to use the potty, as Lewis says, it'll help you "feel free, if you're stuck or something."
